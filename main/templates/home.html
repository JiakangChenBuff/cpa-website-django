What We Do

Engage for Change is a news site that creates content specifically for young people.

Our mission is to educate young people about the world around them in order to encourage engaged citizenship and promote youth empowerment.

About Us

Rhapsody4Youth is a high school student-run 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Our mission is to interact with young individuals by motivating them to articulate their ideas and self-advocate for positive changes in their lives by providing services, such as classes, seminars, workshops, podcasts, a YouTube channel, and volunteering opportunities. We believe it is important to engage the youth in thoughtful conversations and discussions. Rhapsody4Youth's goal is to make us heard.

Learn more about Rhapsody4Youth here.
AV Web is a high school club based in Amador Valley High School, where they teach students Web Development through projects. The Amador Valley Web Development Club is dedicated to providing an easy entry environment for high schoolers interested in web development. We help strengthen the web development skills of our members by providing useful resources and building real-world web applications with the purpose of benefiting our school and the local community.
