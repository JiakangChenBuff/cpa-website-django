About Us

Engage for Change is a news organization that creates content specifically for young people. Our mission is to educate young people about the world around them in order to encourage engaged citizenship and promote youth empowerment.

Meet the Team

Rhapsody for Youth is a high school student-run 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Our mission is to interact with young individuals by motivating them to articulate their ideas and self-advocate for positive changes in their lives by providing services, such as classes, seminars, workshops, podcasts, a YouTube channel, and volunteering opportunities. We believe it is important to engage the youth in thoughtful conversations and discussions. Rhapsody for Youth is to make us heard. Learn more about Rhapsody for Youth here
